**Landlord. Jeremi Wiśniowiecki. Part I**

Jeremi Wiśniowiecki, the hero of the first part of the symphony, was Ukrainian landlord of the 15th century,
implementing new economic laws of development, which resulted in a creation of more than 50 towns.
His image is depicted by the use of minimalist technic of composition, showing the development of the whole music matter from the small rhythmic and intonation impulse.
