Here's a quick synopsis of the idea behind the "Shadow of the Rain" VR application:

"Shadows of the Rain" is an immersive music experience, investigating the potential of artificial imagination to interpret human-created emotion. The project is based on a cover piano music track to the song with the same title and thus has a verbal representation of the emotional landscape of music composition.

Composers: Valentyna Shvets, Anna Shvets
