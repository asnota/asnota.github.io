Here's a quick synopsis of the idea behind the "Omega" VR application:

"Omega" introduces a new genre in Music XR – volumetric music composition, where the user interacts with the structure of the composition.
The work was inspired by the semantic duality of the Messier 17 nebula's names (Omega and Swan),
which reflect the essence of human existence – the aspiration to eternal beauty and the finality of life.
Through interaction with the nebula's visual shapes, users co-create an audio and visual universe in a transcendent VR experience.
